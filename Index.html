Recruitment & Hosting

Why Businesses Choose This

+

Hiring new employees is costly — not just in salaries, but in recruitment time, onboarding, training, benefits, and compliance admin. For many businesses, this slows growth. Our hosting model flips that on its head.

How It Works in Detail

+
  1. Needs Analysis: We arrange a consultation to understand your operational needs, industry requirements, and compliance targets.
  2. Candidate Sourcing: We select from our pool of pre-screened learners who are already enrolled in accredited learnership programs relevant to your industry.
  3. Placement: Learners join your team for 6–12 months, gaining experience while contributing to your operations.
  4. Management & Reporting: We handle contracts, learner support, and compliance documentation.

What’s Included in Our Hosting Service

+
  • End-to-end recruitment of learners
  • Pre-screening for skills, attitude, and fit
  • Coordination of training with accredited providers
  • Monthly progress tracking and issue resolution

Your Business Gains

+
  • Cost Savings: No salary costs — only an affordable monthly admin fee.
  • Compliance Boost: Skills Development and Employment Equity scorecard improvement.
  • Talent Pipeline: Many hosted learners transition into full-time roles post-program.
  • Positive Brand Impact: Contribute to youth employment and skills growth.

Frequently Asked Questions

What’s the catch — how are learners free?

+

Learners are funded through government-backed accredited learnership programs. Your only cost is the admin fee, which covers all recruitment, management, and compliance work.

Do we choose the learners?

+

Yes. We pre-screen candidates and present you with profiles to select from.

Will learners be productive?

+

Yes — learners are matched to roles that suit their training, and we work closely with you to ensure they contribute meaningfully.

WPS manages all learner support and will replace unsuitable candidates if necessary.
